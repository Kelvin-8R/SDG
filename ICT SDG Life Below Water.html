SDG 14 Life Below Water

1. What is Life Below Water SDG

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development

2. The significant about Life Below Water

Oceans are home to seahorses, dolphins, whales, corals, and many other living creatures. Oceans are our planet's life support as they provide water, food and help regulate the weather

3. The Challenges

Without careful management, we could threaten 3 billion people's primary source of protein

4. Potential solution

  1. Stop using plastic bags
  2. Reduce Waste
  3. Use Less Energy
